## March 9, 2026 — New Feature

### Referral Rewards

Invite friends to join Starrise AI and earn rewards when they top up. The more you invite, the more you earn.

<img src="https://mintcdn.com/13108701256/JP9Y9fpeKMNPcUfi/images/changelog/invite/invite.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=JP9Y9fpeKMNPcUfi&q=85&s=61bf72e612cc60f470011d62fdc88a5b" alt="Referral Rewards Panel" width="1852" height="804" data-path="images/changelog/invite/invite.png" />

**How to use:**

1. Go to the [Top Up page](https://ai.alad.com/console/topup) and scroll to the bottom to find the **Referral Rewards** section
2. Copy your unique referral link and share it with friends
3. Once a friend registers via your referral link and completes a top-up, you'll receive a reward
4. Use the transfer function to move your reward balance into your main account balance

**Reward Details:**

* You receive a reward after your friend completes their first top-up
* Rewards are held as pending credits and transferred to your main balance upon request
